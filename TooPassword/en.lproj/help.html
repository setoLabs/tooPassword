▷Import 1Password 4 keychain from Dropbox

  1. Ensure that the keychain is stored in Dropbox.
    1. Open up the preferences of 1Password on your Mac.
    2. Switch to the 'Sync' tab.
    3. Select 'Dropbox' as the storage location for your keychain.
  2. Touch the 'Import from Dropbox' button in tooPassword.
  3. Select the 1Password keychain in the directory listing.

▷Import 1Password 4 keychain from iTunes

  1. Ensure that the keychain is stored in a folder.
    1. Open up the preferences of 1Password on your Mac.
    2. Switch to the 'Sync' tab.
    3. Select 'Folder' as the storage location for your keychain.
  2. Connect your iOS device with iTunes on your Mac.
  3. Open up the synchronization overview in iTunes.
  4. Switch to the 'Apps' tab.
  5. Down below you will see the 'File Sharing' section.
  6. In this section add your keychain to tooPassword.
  7. Touch the 'Import from iTunes' button in tooPassword.
  8. Select the 1Password keychain in the directory listing.

▷Import 1Password 4 keychain from WebDAV

  1. Ensure that the keychain is stored in a folder.
    1. Open up the preferences of 1Password on your Mac.
    2. Switch to the 'Sync' tab.
    3. Select 'Folder' as the storage location for your keychain.
  2. Upload your keychain to your WebDAV server.
  3. Touch the 'Import from WebDAV' button in tooPassword.
  4. Enter your credentials for your WebDAV server.
  5. Select the 1Password keychain in the directory listing.

▷Import 1Password 3 keychain from Dropbox

  1. Ensure that the keychain is stored in Dropbox.
    1. Open up the preferences of 1Password on your Mac or PC.
    2. Switch to the 'General' tab.
    3. Press the button to use Dropbox as the storage location for your keychain.
  2. Touch the 'Import from Dropbox' button in tooPassword.
  3. Select the 1Password keychain in the directory listing.

▷How to find your 1Password keychain

The 1Password keychain has the filename extension .agilekeychain. The default locations are:

  • OS X Mac App Store releases:
    ~/Library/Containers/com.agilebits.onepassword-osx-helper/Data/Documents/
  • OS X AgileBits Online Store releases:
    ~/Library/Application Support/1Password/
  • Windows XP:
    C:\Documents and Settings\your-name\My Documents\1Password\
  • Windows Vista, 7:
    C:\Users\your-name\Documents\1Password\

Note: 1Password 4 doesn't store the agilekeychain file in a default location. You have to use the 'Sync' setting in the preferences.

▷iTunes Import using Windows

If you want to import the keychain with the PC version of iTunes, it's not possible to import the keychain directly. You have to put the keychain in a zip archive first.
