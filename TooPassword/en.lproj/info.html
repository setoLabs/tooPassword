About tooPassword

tooPassword offers read-only access to 1Password keychains and will never modify this file. Cryptographic operations use standard iOS libraries (CommonCrypto) to ensure latest security standards.

Privacy Policy

tooPassword does not collect any personal information. In case you use third-party cloud synchronization services, please refer to the corresponding privacy policy of the provider.
